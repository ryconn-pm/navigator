Scheduling Inspections

Complete these steps in order each scheduling cycle to assign and notify tenants of upcoming inspections.

1

Get "to be scheduled" list

Trigger the Power Automate flow to pull the list of units that need to be scheduled for inspection.

2

Upload data to calendar visualizer

Open the Inspection Schedule Calendar and upload the latest scheduling data.

Open calendar
3

Change custom fields in AppFolio

Update the custom inspection fields for each unit in AppFolio to reflect the new scheduled dates.

Open AppFolio
4

Refresh inspection dates — automation report

Open the Inspection Dates automation report sheet in SharePoint and refresh the data.

Open spreadsheet
5

Send initial scheduling email

Trigger the Power Automate flow to send the initial inspection scheduling email to tenants.
